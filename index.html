About Enprico

Enprico is an online French learning platform dedicated to helping students achieve their immigration goals to Canada and France. Our qualified tutors tailor each lesson to your level and learning pace, guiding beginners to advanced students toward strong results in TEF and TCF exams.

Our mission is to provide practical and structured French training for Express Entry and residency programs. We believe that learning French should be engaging, personalized, and focused on real results.
