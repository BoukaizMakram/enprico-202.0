About Enprico

Empowering learners worldwide to achieve French fluency for TEF & TCF exams through personalized education

Our Mission

To provide practical and structured French training for Canada and France immigration. We believe that language should never be a barrier to achieving your dreams, and we're committed to helping our students succeed in TEF and TCF exams through personalized, effective learning experiences.

Our Story

Enprico was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to help learners achieve their French language goals for immigration to Canada and France. We recognized that many aspiring immigrants struggle to find quality, focused French training for TEF and TCF exams.

Our founders, experienced educators and language specialists, set out to create something different. They envisioned a platform where students could learn at their own pace, with dedicated tutors who understand the specific requirements of immigration exams and programs like Express Entry.

Today, Enprico has grown into a community of learners from around the world, all working toward their dream of living in Canada or France. We've helped students achieve the CLB scores they need, pass their TEF and TCF exams, and build the French skills necessary for life and work abroad.

But we're just getting started. Every day, we work to improve our platform, expand our tutor network, and develop new ways to make French learning more engaging and effective. Our commitment to our students remains unchanged: personalized education that delivers real results for your immigration journey.
